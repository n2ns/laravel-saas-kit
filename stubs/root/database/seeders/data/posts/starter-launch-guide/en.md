Use this starter article as a file-based content example for a new product site.

The directory format is:

- `meta.json` for publishing metadata, topic tags, SEO keywords, pinning, and related links.
- `{locale}.md` files for localized article bodies.

Replace this article with launch notes, onboarding content, or product education before publishing the site.
